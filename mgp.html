Introduction

Welcome to the first season of our Mentoring Program. We aim to offer personalized support to students from the Global South who are interested in starting a career in evolutionary biology. Our mentors are recent graduates or have experience in the field. They will be matched with mentees who are interested in pursuing a master's or PhD degree, or seeking industry positions in the field.

The program provides a safe space for networking and building relationships with individuals who understand the obstacles that students from Global South are facing. Mentees are encouraged to seek insights from our pool of mentors , and the program's focus is on demand-based mentorship, where mentees define the frequency and topic of each session, and mentors offer up-to-date help and support during the application process.

This is the beta version of our program, and we are currently focusing on developing the one-to-one mentoring aspect. However, we aspire to include thematic sessions in future versions, such as how to make an application attractive to schools in Europe and the US. Additionally, we aim to create a lively online community of mentees and mentors beyond each iteration's duration.

The Evolutionary Biology Crash Course team is excited to welcome you on this new journey and looks forward to the success of the mentoring program.
