We created an Evolutionary Biology Crash Course to help prospective students achieve a better understanding of Evolutionary Biology, thereby increasing their chances to access high level graduate programmes in the field.

We are trying to accommodate for students in different time zones, prioritising those in the Global South, so live Q&A sessions will be held twice a day. The course is free and there are no entry requirements, other than an interest for Evolutionary Biology. Keep in mind, however, that the course is designed for undergraduate students with a background in Biological Sciences. We will hand out certificates of completion to those students that assist to at least 50% of Q&A sessions and hand in all of the practical exercises.

Our first edition will be held online from July 13rd to 17th, 2022. You can register here!
